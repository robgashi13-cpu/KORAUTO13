Support availability

01

Choose a car

Browse Copart and IAAI inventory by make, model, year, or VIN and shortlist the lots that match your target.

→ Use filters and VIN search to surface better-value lots faster.
02

Calculate the full price

Estimate the final landed cost with auction fees, inland transport, shipping, customs duty, and VAT.

→ Final cost = bid price + fees + transport + duty + VAT.
03

We bid for you

Set your maximum bid in the account and our team participates in the live auction on your behalf.

→ Your bid cap stays controlled while we execute bidding.
04

Documents processing

After winning, we prepare purchase paperwork and export documents required for legal shipment.

→ You do not need to manage document flow manually.
05

Ocean shipping

The vehicle is moved from the US port to Europe via container or RoRo based on route and vehicle type.

→ Typical transit timeline is around 6 to 8 weeks.
06

Customs clearance

We complete customs procedures and coordinate required declarations to clear the vehicle properly.

→ Our specialists handle clearance end to end.
07

Delivery or pickup

Choose final delivery to your city or pickup from port logistics locations when available.

→ You get clear next steps once the car is released.
